Frequently Asked Questions

Augustinian Computer Society is a program department for Bachelor of Science in Information Technology students at Colegio San Agustin - Bacolod.

In the BSIT program, you can learn a wide range of topics including programming languages like Java and C++, web development, database management, networking, cybersecurity, software engineering principles, and more.

Enrolling in Colegio San Agustin - Bacolod means investing in a high-quality education that prepares you for a successful career in Information Technology. We are committed to your growth, both academically and professionally, and look forward to welcoming you to our community.
